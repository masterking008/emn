WHAT IS EMN?

Eureka! Mentorship Network (EMN) is a flagship initiative of Eureka! 2025, connecting early-stage startups with experienced mentors for strategic guidance, industry insights, and hands-on support. EMN's mission is to foster innovation, accelerate growth, and build scalable ventures through curated mentor-matching and personalized sessions.

MISSION & VISION

Mission

To empower startups by providing continuous, high-impact mentorship and building a collaborative entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Vision

To be the leading mentorship network in Asia, nurturing innovation and driving sustainable business growth.
